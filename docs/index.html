The Problem

Food businesses face a daily trade-off: produce too much and waste increases, produce too little and customers walk away. Demand shifts with weekday seasonality, holidays, and weather—making simple averages unreliable.

Overproduction becomes waste

Unsold products represent avoidable cost and unnecessary resource use.

Underproduction loses sales

Stockouts reduce revenue and damage customer trust.

Demand is complex

Calendar signals and weather interact in ways that basic methods miss.

Our Approach

We build a forecasting pipeline that combines time-aware validation with sales lag features and robust tree-based models, producing predictions that are practical for planning.
