A Positive Impact

Accurate forecasts support better daily decisions. When production is closer to real demand, businesses can reduce unsold stock while maintaining availability for customers.

Environmental

Lower overproduction reduces waste and the resources used to produce and dispose of unsold items.

Economic

Fewer losses from unsold goods and fewer missed sales during high-demand periods.

Operational

More stable planning for production, ordering, and staffing—less reactive decision-making.
